Fostering a Global Community

Welcome to the serene beaches of Blue Tide Chapel. Here, we gather to find peace, reflect, and connect with one another in a calming seaside setting. While our chapel exists online, we invite you to envision the tranquil atmosphere of the beach as a reminder of the peace and unity we aim to create together.

Our mission is to create a community where everyone feels welcomed and finds solace. Whether it's participating in virtual meditations or sharing reflections with others, we are here to help you experience the beauty and tranquility that this beach symbolizes.
